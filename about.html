About Me

My name is James Marshall, I create all kinds of digital content. My main focus is developing software, from websites and applications to complex backend systems to interactive video games.

I'm constantly learning and making, in some form or another, exploring new ideas or ways to better improve old ones. Over time I've accrued an array of creative skills in animation, design, visual effects and more.
